I spent most of my childhood living in Toronto, with my parents and my brother. I've got four siblings, three sisters and one brother but my three sisters grew up with their mom. I loved growing up in Toronto I lived fairly far from the central core but I still loved looking out at the skyline at night. I've spent a lot of time looking out at that skyline, listening to music, or playing music. I play bass and also like to make a bit of music in cakewalk occasionally.

Just this year however I've left Toronto and moved out to Halifax to pursue a university degree in computer science. It was a hard decision to make and an emotional departure from Toronto but I love it here. I'm very lucky to be here, and to be able to have traded out my Toronto skyline view for the beautiful library view. A view that I am actually enjoying as this website is being created.
